Chicken Biryani
Ingredients
  • Chicken pieces
  • Basmati rice
  • Onions, tomatoes, ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices, herbs
  • Yogurt
Method
  1. Marinate chicken and cook with spices and vegetables.
  2. Layer rice and chicken, cook together till done.
Advantage: Rich taste, aromatic spices. Provides proteins and carbs.
Disadvantage: Time-consuming, high calorie.
Chocolate Cake
Ingredients
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa powder
  • Baking powder & soda
  • Eggs, milk, oil
Method
  1. Mix dry and wet ingredients.
  2. Bake at 180°C for 35 minutes.
Advantage: Popular dessert, tasty treat.
Disadvantage: High sugar, high calorie.
Ice Cream
Ingredients
  • Milk, cream
  • Sugar
  • Flavor (vanilla, chocolate)
Method
  1. Mix ingredients, churn and freeze till solid.
Advantage: Cools body, enjoyable treat.
Disadvantage: High fat and sugar
